My Story

For most of my life, my brain was wrapped around swimming. I started swimming competitively at 6, and swam all the way through college. After graduating from UW-Eau Claire, I didn't feel a strong career pull toward my history degree, and instead opted to follow what I was passionate about. Swimming.

After a few years, I left my home state of Minnesota to take a full time coaching job down in Houston. It was a fantastic experience for me and I was able to craft so many meaningful connections with my athletes. However, as much as I loved coaching, I decided I was ready to push myself into a new challenge.

My dad worked in software development for IBM for over three decades, so I always had some interest in programming, even if it wasn't my personal career path. I began seeing advertisements for coding bootcamps, until I came across DigitalCrafts' Full Stack Immersive Program. In October 2019, I decided that I would leave my full time coaching job in order to go all in on the bootcamp. I will graduate from the bootcamp in May 2020 and I'm excited to continue down this new career path.
